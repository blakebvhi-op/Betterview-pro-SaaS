Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

1. Who we are

Better View Pro ("the app") is a private, internal-use customer relationship management and job-scheduling application operated by Better View Home Improvements LLC ("we", "us"), W141N5974 Kaul Ave, Menomonee Falls, WI 53051, phone (414) 234-3700.

The app is used exclusively by Better View Home Improvements employees and authorized agents to manage leads, estimates, invoices, scheduling, and related business operations.

2. Information we collect

The app collects and stores the following categories of information:

  • Customer information entered by our team: names, contact phone numbers, email addresses, physical service addresses, project notes, and the contents of estimates and invoices we prepare for those customers.
  • Operational data: job schedules, work orders, material orders, internal tasks, and team assignments.
  • Authentication data: email addresses and hashed credentials for authorized employees who log into the app.
  • QuickBooks integration data: when authorized by an account owner, the app stores OAuth tokens used to read invoice and customer records from, and write invoices to, the connected QuickBooks Online company.
  • Website lead intake: when a visitor submits the contact form on our website, the submission (name, contact info, and project description) is stored in the app.

3. How we use this information

We use the information solely to operate our home-improvement business: contacting prospective and existing customers, preparing and tracking estimates and invoices, scheduling work, communicating internally, and meeting tax and accounting obligations.

4. How we share this information

We do not sell or rent customer information. The app shares data only with:

  • Supabase (our database and backend hosting provider) for storage.
  • Intuit / QuickBooks Online, when an account owner has authorized the integration, for the purpose of synchronizing invoice and customer data with the connected QuickBooks company.
  • Resend, when configured, for sending lead notification emails to our staff.
  • Government agencies or courts, only when legally required.

5. QuickBooks data specifically

When the QuickBooks integration is active, the app uses Intuit's official OAuth flow and accesses only the data necessary for invoice and customer synchronization. The app never stores raw QuickBooks credentials. Tokens are stored encrypted at rest in our database and may be revoked at any time by the account owner from within the app or from the QuickBooks "Apps" settings.

6. Data retention

We retain customer and operational records as long as they are useful for our business or required by applicable record-keeping law. Account owners can delete records from within the app at any time.

7. Security

The app enforces user authentication, role-based access controls, and row-level security at the database level. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We are a small business and do not claim to operate to enterprise security standards; users should not store information in the app that they would not store in any other small-business CRM.
